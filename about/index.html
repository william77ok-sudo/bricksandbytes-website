About the studio

Building the useful layer between an idea and its impact.

Bytes & Bricks is an independent product studio creating practical AI tools, automations and SaaS ventures for modern work.

Our point of view

Technology should make the next step clearer.

We build products around genuine problems—not technology for its own sake. That means listening carefully, defining the smallest useful solution and improving it through real feedback.

Our portfolio spans founder decision support, financial organisation and lightweight operational monitoring. Alongside our own ventures, we selectively develop client and partner products where the problem, purpose and people are a strong fit.
